Under the Sands


How to play: The toy is a classic arkanoid. The rules are straightforward - arm yourself with your mouse to move the platform at the bottom of the screen. Hit the blocks on the screen with a ball and try to put a platform under it in time to direct it to the right place. Do not let the ball fly by - as a result, you will lose one of your lives. The game will only be completed when all the blocks on the screen that can be destroyed are broken.
